# Indicator of Compromise (IOC) Analysis

## Objective
Identify and analyze Indicators of Compromise using threat intelligence platforms.

## IOC Investigated
IP Address: 185.220.101.45

## Tools Used
- VirusTotal
- AlienVault OTX

## Analysis
The IP address was searched in multiple threat intelligence platforms to determine its reputation and potential malicious activity.

## Findings
The IP is associated with TOR network infrastructure, which is sometimes used by attackers to hide their origin.

## Conclusion
IOC analysis helps SOC analysts quickly determine whether an IP address or domain is associated with suspicious or malicious activity.
